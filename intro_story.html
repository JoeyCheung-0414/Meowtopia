My Dear Hero,

Hello hero, This is a summons from me, the king of Meowtopia. Hero we are in a dire situation and we need your help. The citizens need you and our kingdom is in chaos. Our Meowsionary Mittens has had a vision that only the bravest of cats, you, our chosen hero may save the day.

The beloved King of Meowtopia,
Leo
