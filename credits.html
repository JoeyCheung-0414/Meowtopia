Hello Hero!

I'm Joey Cheung, the creator of this website. I hope you enjoy your adventure! This is created for my Art 74 class at SJSU. My idea was to just make a simple short choose your own adventure. The shield designs you saw today are my old designs that were originally a real-life tiny sculpture turned illustration and now turned into 3d models.
