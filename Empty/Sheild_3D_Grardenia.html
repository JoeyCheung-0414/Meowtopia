Gardenia’s Gift

Gardenia’s Gift, is the shield and viewed with the power of the queen of the Fay titania. It was said that Meow Smith who created the shield was close with the fairy queen so his crafted shields often have the gift of mother nature—being able to grow plants of all kinds. gardenia’s gift was the Meow Smith, the finest piece before his death.

Meowtopia flora is suffering and slowly dwindling. For unknown reasons, the kingdom plants and farm life have been slowly dying over the past few months. Farmers and biologists have been trying to work for new fertilizers and everything they can but in the end, they couldn’t figure out, and now people are starving, hungry, and angry.
